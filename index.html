OUTDONE
MARKETING

"Revenue systems for contractors who are tired of word-of-mouth."

Outdone Marketing is an Alberta-incorporated agency specializing in database reactivation (DBR) and outbound marketing for contractors and small businesses. We build automated lead generation and reactivation systems using GoHighLevel, n8n, and multi-channel outreach — helping roofers, exterior service companies, and home service businesses convert dormant leads into booked jobs.

Current focus: cold outbound to Canadian roofing contractors using review-blast offers as proof-of-concept, with a 14-day free trial funnel and SMS/cold email sequences targeting Google Business Profile data.

TRP
MAINTENANCE

"Where operational discipline meets field execution."

TRP Maintenance (formerly TRP Build Co) was a residential maintenance and field service operation built to establish systems for client acquisition, quoting, scheduling, and delivery. Operating as the hands-on precursor to Outdone Marketing, it provided the real-world feedback loop needed to build effective outbound and reactivation systems.

The processes, scripts, and frameworks developed through TRP directly informed Outdone's current service offerings — particularly around lead follow-up sequences and contractor-specific sales language.

Field Operations Systems Building Client Acquisition Calgary, AB
Key Learnings
Client Reactivation
Dormant clients respond 3–5x better to personalized SMS than email — insight now core to DBR methodology at Outdone.
Outbound Scripts
Contractor-specific language and objection handling built from real sales calls — no fluff, just booked jobs.
Systems Over Services
Pivoting from delivery to building the system that delivers — the foundational philosophy behind Outdone.

Designing at the intersection of business and craft

I'm a third-year Global Business and Digital Arts student at the University of Waterloo — a program that blends UX design, business strategy, and digital media. My work lives at the intersection of how things look and how things work.

Outside of school, I run Outdone Marketing — an Alberta-incorporated agency building automated outbound and reactivation systems for contractors across Canada. I'm hands-on with GoHighLevel, n8n, Supabase, and cold outreach infrastructure, which gives my design work an unusually operational edge.

I'm drawn to projects with real stakes: brand redesigns that need to move product, apps that need to retain users, and systems that need to scale without breaking. I care about the outcome, not just the output.

When I'm not designing or building systems, I'm cooking with ancestral nutrition principles, running a homelab media server, or trying to improve my chess game from the beginner ranks.
